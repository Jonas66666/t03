Understanding Energy Ratings

In Australia, the Energy Rating Label helps consumers compare the energy efficiency of appliances. The more stars, the more energy efficient the appliance is compared to other models of similar size.

Our televisions range from 4 to 6 stars, ensuring you get the most efficient technology available on the market.

Our Mission

EcoPower TVs was established in 2015 with a simple mission: to provide Australian consumers with high-quality televisions that minimize energy consumption without compromising on performance or features.

We believe that enjoying premium entertainment shouldn't come at the cost of high electricity bills or excessive environmental impact.

Our Commitment to Energy Efficiency

We work closely with manufacturers to ensure all our televisions meet the highest energy efficiency standards. We're proud to offer products that are:

  • Designed for low power consumption
  • Certified with high energy star ratings
  • Equipped with smart power-saving features
  • Compliant with Australian energy efficiency standards
